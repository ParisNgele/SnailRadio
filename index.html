Jacaranda FM, founded in 1986, is a popular South African radio station broadcasting in English and Afrikaans. Based in Gauteng, it offers a mix of music, news, and entertainment, focusing on contemporary hits and classic favorites.

KEXP USA

KEXP is a renowned public radio station based in Seattle, USA. Founded in 1972, its known for eclectic mix of music, featuring genres ranging from indie rock, hip-hop, and world music. KEXP’s mission is to support and promote independent artists.

WFMU FREEFORM

WFMU is a freeform, listener-supported radio station that is known for its eclectic and experimental programming. The station covers a wide range of genres including rock, jazz, punk, R&B, electronica, and much more.

FRANCE INTER

France Inter is a major French public radio station, part of the France Télévisions network. Founded in 1963, it provides a mix of news, culture, talk shows, and music. Known for its comprehensive and news coverage.

RADIO MONSTER FM

Radio Monster FM is an online radio station that specializes in playing electronic dance music (EDM), including genres such as house, techno, trance, and other electronic-based styles. The station is designed for people who enjoy high-energy music, typically popular among fans of club music and festival vibes.

FIP RADIO

FIP Radio (also known as France Inter Paris or FIP), is a French public radio station known for its eclectic music programming. Established in 1971, FIP offers a mix of genres, including jazz, classical, rock, world music, and electronic, making it a great choice for listeners who enjoy a wide variety of sounds.

NME RADIO

NME Radio is an online radio station launched in 2008 by the British music magazine NME (New Musical Express). It focuses on playing new and alternative music, including indie, and rock. The station is part of NME's efforts to expand beyond its print magazine and digital content, offering music and interviews with artists.
